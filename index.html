Hammas Bin Tanveer

I work on network performance at SpaceX Starlink and completed a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Iowa’s SPARTA Lab (advised by Prof. Rishab Nithyanand). My research spans IPv6 scanning and censorship measurement, and empirical analysis of satellite networking systems.
